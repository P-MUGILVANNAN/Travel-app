Amazon Rainforest, Brazil:

The Amazon Rainforest, the world's largest tropical rainforest, spans South America and is renowned for its immense biodiversity, hosting thousands of plant and animal species. Visitors can enjoy unique experiences, including guided wildlife tours, river cruises, and interactions with indigenous communities, all set within this vibrant and dense ecosystem.

Serengeti National Park, known for its incredible wildlife and dramatic landscapes, is a prime destination for safari enthusiasts.
