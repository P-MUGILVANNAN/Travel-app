Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Nestled in the Himalayas, Manali offers stunning landscapes, adventure activities, and hiking trails such as the Hampta Pass and Beas Kund treks.

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Known as the yoga capital of the world, Rishikesh is also a gateway to the Himalayas. You can enjoy hiking in nearby areas like the Neer Garh Waterfall trek and the Kunjapuri Temple trek.

Nainital, Uttarakhand

This picturesque hill station is surrounded by mountains and offers various nature walks and hikes, including the trek to Naina Peak for panoramic views.

Munnar, Kerala

Famous for its tea gardens and rolling hills, Munnar has several hiking trails, including the Anamudi Peak trek, which is the highest point in South India.

Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh

A beautiful town in the Northeast, Tawang is surrounded by stunning mountains and monasteries. The Sela Pass trek offers breathtaking views and an adventurous experience.

Kanha National Park, Madhya Pradesh

Known for its rich wildlife, Kanha offers opportunities for jungle safaris and nature walks, allowing visitors to explore the park's stunning landscapes and diverse flora and fauna.
